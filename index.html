Why play here?

Epic scale

Up to 200 players for a map that stays alive — busy hotspots, roaming packs, and constant encounters.

Fair gameplay

No mixpacking, no overpacking, no body denying. Active moderation against cheaters and exploit abuse.

OCE-first

Built for Australian & New Zealand time zones with stable restarts and a community that’s awake when you are.
