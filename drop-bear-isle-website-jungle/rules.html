Core rules (quick read)

No cheating or exploits

Anything that gives unfair advantage (hacks, glitches, dupes, exploit spots) is a fast-track to removal.

Play the game, don’t grief

Targeted harassment, spawn camping, or behavior intended only to ruin others’ time is not welcome.

Respect the community

No hate speech, slurs, or personal attacks. Keep it competitive, not toxic.

Follow admin instructions

Argue later in the proper Discord channel—don’t derail live situations.

Use English in global channels

So moderation and players can understand what’s happening. (Private DMs are your business.)

No impersonation

Don’t pretend to be staff, developers, or other players.

Where the “full rules” live

Discord is the source of truth for the most up-to-date rule wording, event rules, and announcements. If something changes, we’ll post it there first.

FAQ
Is this an official server?
No. The Drop Bear Isle is a community-run, unofficial server and Discord. We’re not affiliated with or endorsed by The Isle’s developers/publishers.
Can I appeal a punishment?
Yes — follow the Discord process for appeals. Provide context, logs/screenshots if you have them, and keep it respectful.
Do you wipe on Evrima updates?
Sometimes. Major updates can require wipes/maintenance. We announce any changes ahead of time in Discord.
