Server Info

Everything you need to know about how The Drop Bear Isle is run.

How to Join
Overview
  • Name: The Drop Bear Isle
  • Region: Australia (OCE)
  • Game: The Isle: Evrima
  • Ruleset: Official-style (simple and familiar)
  • Capacity: Up to 200 players
What we’re aiming for

Official-style gameplay, but at a scale that turns every hotspot into a story. Expect big groups, big risks, and bigger third-parties.

What makes us different

High-pop design

Configured for huge concurrency so the world feels alive (and dangerous) at all times.

Minimal rule bloat

We keep it close to official servers. Less “reading”, more playing.

Discord-driven ops

Announcements, events, and support live on Discord, so players aren’t left guessing.

Notes
  • Updates/Wipes: Evrima updates may require maintenance or wipes. We’ll always announce in Discord.
  • Restarts: Scheduled restarts and patches are communicated on Discord to keep gameplay stable.
  • Support: If you get stuck, open a ticket or ask in the appropriate Discord channel.
